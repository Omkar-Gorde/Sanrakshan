Our Cause And Motivation

Protecting Our Past, Empowering Our Present, Building a Better Future!

This is Sanrakshan, Our mission is to preserve India’s historic forts, ensuring their rich heritage and architectural significance are safeguarded for future generations through sustainable conservation efforts. Additionally, we are committed to supporting individuals with syndromes by fostering a compassionate community that prioritizes care, awareness, and inclusivity. Through advocacy, education, and tailored support, we aim to enhance their quality of life and empower them to thrive. To achieve these goals, we actively collaborate with renowned organizations, dedicated experts, and passionate individuals who share our vision. By leveraging collective expertise and resources, we strive to make a lasting and meaningful impact on both cultural heritage and social well-being.
